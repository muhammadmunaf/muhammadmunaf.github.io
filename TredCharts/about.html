About TredCharts

Our Mission

Since the concept phase of our site, we’ve remained determined and focused on the goal of providing exceptional and readable financial data for traders and investors within all financial markets, that way more individuals who use our product can credit themselves with a positive and successful experience.

The Idea

The inception of tred-charts.com was brought up upon an idea to create an observation tool for recording and viewing live and past dated movements of the options chain within a stock. No financial data imposed on any site or program, contains a well constructed feature and or product that gives the user direct quote data of a specifically searched option strike. Under this ability, option traders can see how the premium of a call or put strike has moved throughout the intraday, week, month, and year to date-giving traders an advantage of being able to make quicker and successful option trades(sometimes referred to as scalping) within any desired time frame.

Our Team

The idea of the product within Tredcharts was drafted and founded by Angelo Anton. The name, logo, and the design of the website, TredCharts.com, was constructed by co-founder/owner Max Heltzel. Together, they managed to assemble a smart team of innovators whom put together TREDCHARTS- as we know it today.
